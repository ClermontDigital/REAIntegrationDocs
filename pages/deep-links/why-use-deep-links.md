# Why use deep links

Deeply integrate your CRM with realestate.com.au’s Ignite platform. Allow customers to view campaign reports, upgrade listings, or complete CMAs without leaving their familiar environment.

## Key Benefits

-   **Save time and money:** why spend months building campaign reporting when you can deep link into Ignite’s existing solution? Leverage powerful features instead of building from scratch.
-   **Better user experience:** customers complete tasks quickly in Ignite and return straight to their CRM. No platform switching, no learning curve.
-   **Quick feature access:** provide instant access to Ignite’s feature set through strategic deep links. Connect customers to permissions management to enable new CRM capabilities.

## Example Flows

**Campaign Reporting**

1.  View summary in CRM
2.  Click deep link to full Ignite report
3.  Download analytics
4.  Return to CRM

**Listing Upgrades**

1.  Identify listing in CRM
2.  Click deep link to upgrade interface
3.  Complete upgrade in Ignite
4.  Return with updated status

## Who Benefits?

**CRM Providers:** Add comprehensive real estate tools without building them. Reduce customer need for multiple platforms.

**Agencies:** Access powerful tools from familiar interface. Get full realestate.com.au data ecosystem through existing CRM.

## What features are available?

### Listing performance

Link your customers directly to the performance reporting for a specific listing in Ignite.

Format: `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/listings-and-campaigns/listing/<listingId>/performance/`

What your customers will see:

![](https://partner.realestate.com.au/images/ignite/ignite-listing-performance.png)

### Listing management

Link your customers to the listing management page in Ignite where they can manage and upgrade their listing directly.

Format: `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/listings-and-campaigns/listing/<listingId>/manage/`

What your customers will see:

![](https://partner.realestate.com.au/images/ignite/ignite-listing-manage.png)

### Enquiries

Link your customers directly to their enquiries inbox in Ignite for easy follow up and management of enquiries. If you have a specific ListingID you can link them directly to the enquiries for that listing for more targeted access.

Format:

-   `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/inbox/enquiries`
-   `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/listings-and-campaigns/listing/<listingId>/enquiries`

What your customers will see:

![Ignite enquiries inbox](https://partner.realestate.com.au/images/ignite/ignite-enquiries-inbox.png)

### Prospecting

Link your customers directly to their saved CMAs in Ignite allowing them easy access to the data.

Link format: `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/prospecting/reports/cma`

-   `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/insights/agency-dashboard/lead-insights/commercial`
-   `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/insights/agency-dashboard/performance/residential`

### Self-managed permissions

If your customers need to add additional permissions to enable features in your CRM, you can link them directly to the permissions management page. If you don’t know the AgencyID you can link them to the generic permissions page, and they can select their agency. The majority of agencies have a single workplace so the additional step is often not needed.

Links format:

-   `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/manage/data-and-integrations?agencyId=<agencyId>`
-   `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/manage/data-and-integrations`

What your customers will see:

![Ignite Data and integrations page](https://partner.realestate.com.au/images/ignite/ignite-data-and-integrations.png)

### Explore subscriptions

If your customers need to upgrade their subscription to enable features in your CRM, you can link them directly to the subscriptions management page. From here they can view their current subscription, explore additional products, and upgrade their plan.

Link format: `https://ignite.realestate.com.au/manage/subscription-and-listing-contract`

What your customers will see:

![Ignite subscriptions and listing contract](https://partner.realestate.com.au/images/ignite/ignite-explore-subscriptions.png)

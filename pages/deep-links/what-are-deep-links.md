# What are deep links?

## Introduction

A deep link is a link or button you can place in your app, that lets your users jump directly into a specific part of Ignite. It provides a smooth and uninterrupted user experience, allowing users to navigate directly to relevant content complete the activity they’ve come for and be back in your application quickly.

## How deep links Work

→ User interacts with your application

→ User clicks a deep link to a resource via your application

→ The user is taken directly to the resource in the Ignite interface

→ User completes the task within Ignite

→ User closes the tab

→ The user returns to your application

## Benefits

-   Better user experience
-   Seamless inter-application navigation
-   Smooth and fast access to content
-   Access to the newest features in Ignite without needing to build them yourself
-   Saves on time and build costs without a reduction in user experience

Deep links jump users straight to the content they need, reducing clicks, and letting you control the user journey. Allows your users to jump into Ignite, complete their task, and get back to your application quickly.

## In Action

#### Deep links to Ignite Listings

→ A user is interested in seeing how their listings are performing

→ They click a deep link button in their CRM to view their listing performance in Ignite

→ They are taken directly to the listing performance page in Ignite

→ They review the performance of their listings

→ They close the tab and return to their CRM to continue their work

#### Deep links to Ignite Features

→ A user is interested in seeing their saved CMA reports, how their agent brand is performing, and how to manage their subscriptions in Ignite

→ They click deep link buttons in their CRM to view these features in Ignite

→ They are taken directly to the relevant pages in Ignite

→ They review their saved CMA reports, agent brand performance, and manage their subscriptions

→ They close the tabs and return to their CRM to continue their work
